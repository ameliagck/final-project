Move my locker Use this option to change the location of your assigned locker. This can only be done three times per semester. All locker transfers are immediate. If you are assigned a locker and find that there is already a lock on it, please notify Locker Services. We are located in room E120 on Woodroffe campus. We will remove the lock for you within 1-2 business days, but make sure to bring valid photo ID and your own lock. Algonquin College locker rules and regulations are found here .
